# Marketing Banners Collection

## Banner Templates for Your Services

This folder contains ready-to-use banner templates and guidelines for creating professional marketing materials for your marketplace services.

## Banner Sizes & Specifications

### Square Banner (1080x1080px)
- **Use**: Instagram posts, Facebook posts
- **Template**: `square_banner_template.png`
- **Text guidelines**: Max 20 characters per line, 3-4 lines max

### Story Banner (1080x1920px)
- **Use**: Instagram Stories, Facebook Stories
- **Template**: `story_banner_template.png`
- **Text guidelines**: Max 15 characters per line, 2-3 lines max

### Horizontal Banner (1200x630px)
- **Use**: Facebook cover, Twitter header, website banners
- **Template**: `horizontal_banner_template.png`
- **Text guidelines**: Max 30 characters per line, 2-3 lines max

### Product Banner (800x400px)
- **Use**: Marketplace listings, email headers
- **Template**: `product_banner_template.png`
- **Text guidelines**: Max 25 characters per line, 3-4 lines max

## Design Guidelines

### Color Schemes
- **Professional**: Blue (#2563EB) + White (#FFFFFF) + Gray (#6B7280)
- **Creative**: Purple (#9333EA) + Pink (#EC4899) + White (#FFFFFF)
- **Tech**: Green (#10B981) + Blue (#3B82F6) + Dark (#1F2937)
- **Elegant**: Black (#000000) + Gold (#F59E0B) + White (#FFFFFF)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, sans-serif, 32-48px
- **Subheadings**: Medium, sans-serif, 18-24px
- **Body Text**: Regular, sans-serif, 14-16px

### Layout Principles
1. **Hierarchy**: Most important element largest
2. **Contrast**: High contrast for readability
3. **Balance**: Even distribution of elements
4. **Whitespace**: Don't overcrowd the design

## File Naming Convention

Use this naming system for your banners:
`[service_name]_[size]_[version].[extension]`

Examples:
- `web_design_square_v1.png`
- `logo_design_story_v2.jpg`
- `social_media_horizontal_v1.png`

## Testing Your Banners

### A/B Testing Elements
- **Headlines**: Test different wording
- **Colors**: Try different color schemes
- **Images**: People vs. product shots
- **CTAs**: Button text and placement
- **Pricing**: Show vs. hide prices

### Metrics to Track
- **Click-through rate (CTR)**
- **Conversion rate**
- **Engagement rate**
- **Bounce rate**
- **Time on page**

## Legal Considerations

### Image Rights
- Use royalty-free stock photos
- Create your own images when possible
- Credit photographers when required
- Avoid trademarked logos

### Text Claims
- Be truthful about your services
- Avoid misleading statements
- Include disclaimers for results
- Follow advertising guidelines
