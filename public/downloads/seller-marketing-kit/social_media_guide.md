- #[ServiceType] (e.g., #GraphicDesign)
- #[TargetMarket] (e.g., #SmallBusiness)

- #[SpecificService] (e.g., #LogoDesign)
- #[Niche] (e.g., #StartupDesign)

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### NEGATIVE COMMENTS
**Response strategy:**
1. Acknowledge quickly
2. Address concerns professionally
3. Offer solution offline
4. Follow up publicly

**Example response:**
```
Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We're committed to providing excellent service and would like to understand your experience better. Please DM us so we can resolve this directly. üôè
```

### MISTAKE CORRECTIONS
**If you make a mistake:**
1. Acknowledge immediately
2. Explain what happened
3. Share correction plan
4. Rebuild trust
